About Me

I am about to finish my PhD (HKPFS 2019) study at HKUST (May 2024). During my study, I work closely with Prof. Kwang-Ting Cheng, Prof. Zhang Dong, and Prof. Zengqiang Yan. I am currently looking for opportunities in AI and healthcare industry.

My primary research interest are in Federated Learning, Medical Image Analysis, Foundational Federated Learning, and Temporal Health Data Analytics for Wellness. I am aiming to design a future where personalized health analytics, where most of the computations are performed at edge (mobile PC) is possible.
